## Roadmap Champions

### MFA-G

[![Roadmap Champion](https://img.shields.io/badge/Roadmap-Champion-D4AF37?style=flat-square&logo=gitbook&logoColor=white)](certificates/mfa-g-roadmap-champion-2026-07-23.svg)
[![First Flight](https://img.shields.io/badge/contributor-First_Flight-1D76DB?style=flat-square)](https://github.com/MFA-G)

MFA-G delivered the project's first external pull request by isolating the integration adapter
registry and covering its contract. The contribution closed a Community Upgrade Roadmap issue and
made focused integration tests independent and repeatable.

- Contributor: [@MFA-G](https://github.com/MFA-G)
- Contribution: [PR #22 — isolate the adapter registry](https://github.com/schowdary75/moonsconfig/pull/22)
- Roadmap work: [Issue #21](https://github.com/schowdary75/moonsconfig/issues/21)
- Certificate:
  [MOONSCONFIG-RC-2026-0001](certificates/mfa-g-roadmap-champion-2026-07-23.svg)

## Join the Hall of Fame

Start with a [`good first issue`](https://github.com/schowdary75/moonsconfig/issues?q=is%3Aissue+is%3Aopen+label%3A%22good+first+issue%22),
help with a [`help wanted`](https://github.com/schowdary75/moonsconfig/issues?q=is%3Aissue+is%3Aopen+label%3A%22help+wanted%22)
task, or propose a focused improvement. Recognition covers code, documentation, testing,
accessibility, design, issue research, and community support.

If an eligible contribution is missing, submit a
[recognition nomination](https://github.com/schowdary75/moonsconfig/issues/new?template=recognition_nomination.yml).
