## Scoring

- Merged pull request: **10** points
- Roadmap milestone completion: **5** bonus points
- Accepted issue report: **3** points
- Default-branch commit: **1** point

Badge tiers and eligibility are documented in the
[contributor recognition program](RECOGNITION.md).
